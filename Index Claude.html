Data & Backup
📱 Where is my data? Everything lives in your browser's local storage on this device. Updating the HTML file won't erase your history — as long as you open it in the same browser.

⚠️ Data will be lost if you clear browser data, switch browsers, or move to a new device — unless you download a backup first.
Backups download as .json files straight to your Files app on iPhone. Name them by date to keep your history safe.
